How to stand out from the crowd

By Luke Edward Hall

Start to get your work out there as soon as you can. I remember how difficult it was starting out as a designer and trying to get my name and work out into the world, but I soon discovered there are many things you can do to get people to sit up and notice you. For example, keep a blog or online portfolio to highlight your latest work. Also use social media wisely to help build your own personal brand and image.
