Why it’s good to ask questions

By Rachel Arthur

Don’t be afraid to ask questions and seek out what you don’t know. It’s far too easy to get overwhelmed by the big things involved with setting up a new business and forget that you can lean on others to help you. There are a lot of free portals out there as a starting place, but turn to your own network to find the mentors you need and the experts that can give you essential guidance that you’re not expected to already know yourself – from accountants to lawyers and beyond. Above all else, surround yourself with smart people.
