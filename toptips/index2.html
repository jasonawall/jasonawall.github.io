Jack Monroe

Jack Monroe is the author of bestselling cookbook, A Girl Called Jack, teaching people to cook on the lowest of budgets with limited skills and equipment. She is now writing her third cookbook, Cooking On A Bootstrap. She is an author, broadcaster, campaigner, journalist and food writer. She has appeared on political programmes including BBC Question Time, Any Questions, This a Week and Daily Politics. News credits include regular Sky News, BBC Breakfast and Newsnight. She writes for BBC Good Food, The Mirror, the Guardian and the Independent.

Pizza Pilgrims

Pizza Pilgrims was born in 2011, after brothers Thom and James Elliot drove a three wheeled Piaggio Ape back from Italy on a six week “Pizza Pilgrimage” of discovery. Once home, they installed a Neapolitan pizza oven and sold their first pizza on Berwick St Market. Since then, they have traded at a huge variety of events across the country, and opened three pizzerias in central London.

Luke Edward Hall

Central Saint Martins graduate Luke Edward Hall is a London-based artist and designer whose work, which encompasses interior and fabric design, illustration and ceramics, has taken the world of interiors and fashion by storm. Having established his own studio in 2015, he is renowned for his use of daring palettes and is fast becoming the interior design world’s new go-to wunderkind.

Rachel Arthur

As an award-winning business journalist and digital marketing specialist, Rachel Arthur is first-to-market with global insights and information affecting the transformation of the fashion industry. She is the founder of Fashion & Mash – a daily news site dedicated to the crossroads of fashion, digital communications and technology – and a corresponding consulting business working with leading retail and luxury brands. She is known as an expert on the new and the next, from social media strategy to wearable technology, and regularly speaks on such subjects at conferences around the world, including SXSW, CES, Web Summit and Cannes Lions. She also runs a community and event series for the industry under the hashtag #FashMash.
