What to ask yourself before starting up a new business

By Jack Monroe

  1. What is driving me to do this? Never lose sight of why you started your business in the first place. Once you start to see some success it’s easy to lose sight of where it all started or to be tempted to branch out in ways that don’t fit with the ideals you hold dear. Resist! Focus on those passion points, use them to measure new opportunities against and drive your business forward.
  2. Who can I call when I need to talk? When going into a new business venture, always ensure that you are surrounded by a circle of close friends and family. These will be the people who will be there to pick you up from your lows, but also celebrate your highs. 
  3. Would I get on board if this was a friend’s project? Always be objective towards yourself and don’t be shy of being tough on your ideas and output. It’s this type of attitude that will push you to get the best out of yourself.
